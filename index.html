Research

My research focuses on biochemistry and metabolomics, with a particular interest in microbial metabolism and fermentation systems. I use metabolite profiling to elucidate biochemical pathways and functional interactions in complex biological processes. This work contributes to both fundamental understanding and applied developments in biotechnology.
